Our Heritage

A Family Tradition

La Bella Tavola was born in 1987, when Marco Ricci left his home village near Florence and brought with him three things: his grandmother's recipe book, an unshakeable love for food, and a dream to share Italian culture with the world.

Today, the restaurant is run by the second generation of the Ricci family, preserving every original recipe while embracing new inspirations from Italy's rich culinary landscape.

We believe food is more than sustenance — it is memory, celebration, and connection.
